Women's Ministry

Women ministry was founded by our mother Rev Desire Cedric with the vision of raising Proverbs 31 woman with a "Godly character".The ministry has grown to be backbone of the church , raising powerful intercessors and kingdom financers who support te church's projects. Women meet every Thursdays for their meetings,being taught

  • To be powerful prayer warriors and intercessors who stand in the gap for the ministry.
  • To have home cell groups, preach the word and win souls.
  • To be good wives who know their role according to the word of God.
  • To be industrious and self reliant so as to help the family needs.
  • Women also each other diffrent topics such as houseclaning, cooking, proper laundry, health care , how to handle family relationships and many other topics related to women's group.
